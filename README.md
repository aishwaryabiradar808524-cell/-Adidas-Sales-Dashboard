Adidas Sales Analytics Dashboard

1. Project Title / Headline
Adidas Sales Analytics Dashboard: Sales Performance & Profitability Insights
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Adidas sales performance across regions, states, cities, product categories, retailers, and sales channels. The dashboard enables stakeholders to identify revenue drivers, profitability trends, and customer purchasing patterns for data-driven business decisions.

2. Short Description / Purpose
The Adidas Sales Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales, profit, units sold, and product performance across multiple dimensions. It helps business managers, sales teams, and analysts monitor key performance indicators, identify top-performing markets, evaluate retailer effectiveness, and optimize sales strategies.

3. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
- Power BI Desktop – Primary data visualization and dashboard development platform.
- Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Created calculated measures and KPIs.
- Data Modeling – Established relationships among sales, product, retailer, and regional datasets.
- Interactive Visualizations – Slicers, bar charts, pie charts, line charts, KPI cards, and drill-down analysis.
- File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard presentation.

4. Data Source
Source: Adidas Sales Dataset(Kaggle)
The dataset contains Adidas product sales transactions including:
- Sales revenue
- Operating profit
- Units sold
- Product categories
- Retailers
- Sales methods
- Geographic information (Region, State, City)
- Time-based sales records (Year, Quarter, Month)
The data enables comprehensive analysis of Adidas business performance across multiple dimensions.

5. Features / Highlights
Business Problem
Large retail organizations generate massive amounts of sales data across multiple products, regions, and distribution channels. Without a centralized analytics solution, it becomes difficult to answer critical business questions such as:
- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- Which states and cities contribute most to sales?
- Which retailers drive the greatest profit?
- How do sales vary across quarters?
- Which sales channels perform best?
Manual analysis is time consuming and often leads to missed opportunities.

Goal of the Dashboard
The dashboard aims to:
- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Track operating profit and profitability trends.
- Analyze product category performance.
- Compare regional and state-wise sales contributions.
- Evaluate retailer effectiveness.
- Understand customer purchasing channels.
- Support strategic business decision-making through interactive visualizations.
